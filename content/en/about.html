BORN FROM THE DESIRE TO EXPRESS INNER ARTISTRY & CREATIVITY

Inspired by the vibrant district of Bronx in New York City, our products embody diversity and inclusivity.

The boldness of graffiti, blended with the elegance of the New York city skylines, are the DNA of our brand. Yellow, the most luminous color in the spectrum, is our signature – the color of happiness, creativity and the iconic NYC taxis.

The melting pot of diverse cultures in the Bronx district is the environment to be creative. The colors and the textures for Bronx are the source of inspiration that have influenced our products.

Bronx Colors – Urban Cosmetics is for everyone who wants the freedom to identify themselves through makeup.

Vegan friendly

Respecting life, over the last years we have been investing in finding new make-up formulas that are vegan. Products in this category are free of any ingredients of animal origin.

All of our products are also officially certified as cruelty free by PETA: our products are never tested on animals and we strongly support “beauty without bunnies”.
